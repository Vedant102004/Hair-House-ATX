09   FAQ

Common
Questions

Everything you need to know before your first visit

For complex services like balayage, color corrections, or extensions, we highly recommend a complimentary consultation first. This allows Tyler or Toni to assess your hair, discuss your goals, and provide an accurate time and price estimate. For precision cuts, you can book directly.
Balayage services typically take between 3–6 hours depending on your hair length, density, and the complexity of the color work. Tyler takes his time to ensure each piece is perfectly placed — this is not a service to rush. We recommend blocking out your day and enjoying the experience.
Toni specializes in premium hand-tied weft extensions, tape-in extensions, and fusion/keratin bond extensions. All extensions are sourced from ethical, high-quality suppliers and are available in a full range of textures and colors. Consultations are required for all extension services.
We require a 48-hour notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations within 24 hours are subject to a 50% service fee, and no-shows will be charged 100% of the booked service. We understand life happens — please reach out as soon as possible if you need to make changes.
After your service, Tyler or Toni will provide a personalized aftercare plan tailored to your specific color and hair type. Generally: use sulfate-free shampoo, wash in cool water, apply a weekly mask, and avoid excessive heat. We also carry professional retail products in the studio to help maintain your results at home.
