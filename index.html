09   FAQ

Common
Questions

Everything you need to know before your first visit

For complex services like balayage, color corrections, or extensions, we highly recommend a complimentary consultation. This allows Tyler or Toni to assess your hair and provide an accurate time and price estimate. For precision cuts, you can book directly.
Balayage services typically take 3–6 hours depending on your hair length, density, and the complexity of the color work. Tyler takes his time to ensure each piece is perfectly placed.
Toni specializes in premium hand-tied weft extensions, tape-in extensions, and fusion/keratin bond extensions. All extensions are sourced from ethical, high-quality suppliers. Consultations are required for all extension services.
We require 48-hour notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations within 24 hours are subject to a 50% service fee, and no-shows will be charged 100% of the booked service.
Use sulfate-free shampoo, wash in cool water, apply a weekly mask, and avoid excessive heat. Tyler or Toni will provide a personalized aftercare plan after your service.
Yes! We offer The Inner Circle membership program with three tiers: Bronze ($49/mo), Gold ($99/mo), and Platinum ($199/mo). Members receive exclusive discounts, priority booking, complimentary treatments, and much more.
